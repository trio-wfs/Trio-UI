Tooltip

A short contextual label that appears on hover to clarify the purpose of a UI element. Tooltips are always black with white text and include a directional arrow pointing at the trigger.

category Atomic Component verified Production Ready

info Overview

psychology Purpose
Small hover/focus label that supplements an element. Used to explain icons, expand truncated text, or surface keyboard shortcuts.
priority_high Visual Hierarchy
Low — Tooltip is auxiliary information only. Never carries primary content the user needs to act on.
accessibility Accessibility
Anchored via aria-describedby on the target. Appears on focus, not just hover. Stays visible long enough to read; never carries critical-only information.

recommend Best Practices

  • Choose the position that keeps the tooltip visible within the viewport
