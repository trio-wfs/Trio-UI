Chip

Chips help people enter information, make selections, filter content, or trigger actions. They are compact elements that represent an attribute, text, entity, or action.

category Atomic Component menu_book Storybook design_services Zeplin verified Production Ready

info Overview

psychology Purpose
Chips represent compact pieces of information such as filter tags, categories, selections, or status indicators. They make dense data scannable in VMS workflows.
layers Variants
Contained — solid background fill for high emphasis.
Outline — transparent bg with colored border for lower emphasis.
6 semantic colors available in each variant.
accessibility Accessibility
Chips support keyboard interaction for selection and deletion. The cancel icon on removable chips is focusable and labeled for screen readers. Min touch target is 24px (sm).
